How old do I have to be to get my permit?

You must be 15 and 1/2 years of age or older. If you are over 18, you can take the behind the wheel DMV licensing test after completing a driver's education course and at least 6 hrs. Behind the Wheel training.

How old do I have to be to get a license?

After completing a driver's education course and 6 hours behind the wheel training, you must be at least 16 years old to get your license.

How old do I have to be to take Driver's Ed.?

You must be at least 15 years old.

How do I take Driver's Ed.?

You can either take Drivers's Education in class at Driller's Driving School or you can enroll in online Driver's Education. If you are taking in class Driver's Ed., you must complete a combination of two Saturdays and two Sundays to earn the DMV required credits (Classes run Saturday to Sunday, 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM). Visit our Prices Page for pricing information.

How do I receive my Driver's Education certificate?

You must come in person to the Driller's Driving School location at 1326 H St. Suite #4 and pick it up. Please bring some form of identification.

What do I need to take my permit test?

You must have/do the following during your visit to the DMV:
  • Driver's Education completion certificate
  • A license/permit application (DL 44)
  • Birth Certificate
  • Social Security Number
  • Pay a nonrefundable application fee ($33.00)

What is "Behind the Wheel Training"?

A licensed and experienced Driller's Driving School instructor will teach you how to drive on a predetermined route for 2 hours. California law requires at least 6 hours behind the wheel instruction by a DMV certified instructor in order to apply for a license. We provide the car that you will be driving in.

Do we offer pick-up service?

Unfortunately, Drillers Driving School does not offer pick-up service. Students will be required to run the designated course beginning and ending at Drillers Driving School (1326 H St. Suite #4)

Do I need to wear glasses?

If your permit designates that you need corrective lenses, you must come to the behind the wheel with them on. A $75.00 "no-show" fee will be charged if you are unable to acquire suitable corrective lenses within 15 minutes of your appointment.

I lost my permit. Do I need it for the behind the wheel?

Yes. While you are unlicensed, you can ONLY drive with a valid permit and an accompanying licensed adult aged 21 and older.
