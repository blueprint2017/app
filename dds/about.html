About Drillers Driving School

Drillers Driving School has been in business since 1994. Since our foundation nearly 22 years ago, we have labored to provide all our customers with a personal connection to our business, whether it's through affordable prices, an experienced staff, or just a warm welcome. We strive to be the safest and most affordable option for driver's education and driver's training in Bakersfield, principles that we at Drillers Driving School have kept in mind for over two decades, and will keep in mind for many years to come.

Drillers Driving School is licensed by the California Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) and is insured and bonded as required by the DMV.
